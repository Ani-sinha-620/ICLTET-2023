Session & Tracks

Chandigarh Engineering College, Jhanjeri is organizing its 2nd international conference ICLTET 2023. The conference intends to provide a virtual platform to share the vision and experiences of the brilliant minds.

It gives us immense pleasure to invite you all to participate in this conference and share your research findings and innovative ideas to the peer community. Faculties and research scholars are invited to submit original and unpublished research work for presentation at conference ICLTET 2023. Also kindly inform to your Colleagues, Friends, Research Scholars and Students for participation in various events of the conference. The detailed submission guidelines are available at the Conference website.

The work on areas related to the following domains, but not limited to them, are called

  • Track 1 : Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning/Robotics/IOT/ Communication Network/Computing:
    • Machine Learning
    • Artificial Intelligence
    • Robotics
    • Block chain
    • Intelligent Systems for IoT
    • Machine to Machine/Device Communications
    • Network Design and Architecture
    • Wireless Systems and Applications
    • IoT and Big Data
    • Software Engineering
    • VLSI Design, Embedded System
  • Track 2 :Sustainable development/Energy/Mechanical Engineering/Civil Engineering:
    • Mechatronics, Renewable energy, Refrigeration & Air conditioning
    • Manufacturing Technology, Automobile, Electric Vehicle, heat Transfer
    • CAD/CAM
    • Building Materials and Concrete Technology, Construction Technology and Management
    • Geology in Civil Engineering, Structural Analysis and Design
    • Building Planning and Drawing, Surveying in Civil Engineering
    • Geotechnical and Foundation Engineering, Hydrology and Irrigation Engineering
    • Transport Engineering, Estimation and Evaluation
  • Track 3 :Nano-Material Research, Sciences, Environment/Medical sciences/ Business Management & studies:
    • Nano-Materials Research
    • Applied Sciences
    • Environmental and Chemistry
    • Medical Sciences
    • Managing Technology & Sustained Innovations
    • Resource Management & Sustainable Development
